Corolla L (Base Model)

  • Exterior: LED headlights, 15-inch steel wheels with wheel covers.
  • Interior: Fabric-trimmed seats, 60/40 split fold-down rear seat, air conditioning with dust and pollen filter.
  • Standard: Toyota Safety Sense 2.0, Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection, Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist, Road Sign Assist, and Automatic High Beams.
  • Optional: Blind Spot Monitor and Rear Cross-Traffic Alert available on higher trims.

Corolla iM (Hatchback)

  • Exterior: 17-inch alloy wheels, body kit, sporty front fascia and rear bumper.
  • Interior: Sport gauge cluster, dual-zone climate control, leather-wrapped steering wheel.
  • Cargo space: Increased rear cargo space, fold-down rear seats for additional space.
  • Standard: Toyota Safety Sense 2.0, Pre-Collision System, Pedestrian Detection, Lane Departure Alert, Steering Assist, Road Sign Assist, Automatic High Beams.
  • Optional: Blind Spot Monitor, Rear Cross-Traffic Alert available on higher trims.
